Project overview

CivicLens AI

A civic issue reporting and resolution platform.

CivicLens connects citizens and municipal officials in one simple workflow: citizens report public issues with photos and location, while officials review, assign, verify, and close the work from a command dashboard.

About This Project

The goal is to reduce the gap between public complaints and municipal action. The citizen side focuses on fast reporting, clear evidence, and local support. The official side focuses on prioritization, dispatch, proof upload, and closure tracking.

Report

Citizens upload an issue photo, location, and short description.

Act

Officials sort complaints, assign workers, and monitor status.

Verify

Completion photos are uploaded and the ticket is closed.
